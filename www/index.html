Who am I?

My name is Alexander Nunes, I am a 22 year-old who started studying Games Design at Bournemouth University
to fulfill my dream of becoming part of a team who will develop a game that will stay in people's memories
for years to come. I aspire to be a level-designer that can recreate astonishing worlds and spaces that
make the player pull up for a second and admire the beaty of a digital world.
