Who am I?

My name is Alexander Nunes, I am a 22 year-old who started studying Games Design at Bournemouth University
to fulfill my dream of becoming part of a team who will develop a game that will stay in people's memories
for years to come. I aspire to be a level-designer that can recreate astonishing worlds and spaces that
make the player pull up for a second and admire the beauty of a digital world.

My Story


I came to the UK in March of 2018 due to economical issues that my family and I were facing. I was born in
Venezuela but grew up in Portugal, specifically in the Island of Madeira since 2004. Ever since I arrived
we were in an ever-developing situation that improved as time went by. I managed to qualify to BU in just
2 years and a half and study what I loved most: videogames. I possess very few skills in comparisson to my
classmates, but that did not cross me as troublesome one bit since the course is suited for people with no
previous skills. And there you go, that is my story.

Education


I obtained my Level 2s back in Venezuela and got a GSCE equivalent of Maths, due to English being studied
as a second language, it was not valid. To compensate this, a Level 2 in Functional Skills English served
as sufficient to meet any university requirements. My Level 3 consists of a Diploma of Access to Higher
Education in IT Computing that I obtained in 2019 through Bournemouth and Poole College. Finally, in order
to qualify for any university I overtook an online IELTS examination and got a score of 7.9. This proved
that my English profficiency was above the threshold of acceptance.
